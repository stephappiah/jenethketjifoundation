About Us

What We Do As a Charitable Organisation

Making society a better place by shaping children

Jeneth Ketji Foundation seeks to provide basic necessiies to most children and individuals in need of such essential requirements. The vulnerable and less-endowed people require immense supporting aid of which mostly are the basic necessities which include food and healthcare, also to have a bright and quality future in terms of education.

Our Mision

  • Bridgeing the literacy gap in by educating the less-endowed.
  • Sheltering the homeless.
  • Providing basic ammenities such as food and clothing to orphans.
