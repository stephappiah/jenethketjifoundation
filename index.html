Orphans Across Namibia

Housing the Homeless

One of our main reasons Jeneth Ketji foundation was founded was to compensate for the increase in homeless children across Namibia. We're dedicated to establishing orphan shelters to help groom the next generation of kids.

Fighting Inequality through Education

It is our mission to help groom the next generation of orphaned children in Namibia. We want to bridge the inequality gap which prevents these kids from realising their dreams through education.
